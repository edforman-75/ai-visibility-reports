Where HOKA Could Improve

While HOKA has excellent visibility overall, there are categories where the brand is underrepresented.

Gaps Identified

Trail Running

HOKA's road shoes (Bondi, Clifton) are mentioned more than trail shoes (Speedgoat, Challenger). Given HOKA's strong trail heritage, this represents an opportunity to build more trail-focused content.

Racing/Carbon Shoes

Rocket X and Cielo X rarely appear in race-day or speed-focused queries. Nike and other competitors dominate the racing category in AI responses.

Budget-Conscious Queries

HOKA doesn't appear in "best shoe under $150" or similar budget-focused searches. Highlighting value models or previous-season options could capture this segment.

Fashion/Lifestyle

HOKA lifestyle and casual models aren't mentioned in style-focused queries. The brand is positioned purely for performance, missing lifestyle crossover opportunities.
