Strategic Recommendations

1. HOKA: Claim Ultramarathon Category

HOKA is NOT mentioned for ultramarathon queries despite being their heritage and core differentiator.

  • Update product listings to emphasize ultra-distance credentials
  • Highlight trail racing heritage and elite athlete endorsements
  • Ensure product descriptions mention "ultramarathon", "ultra-distance", "100-mile"

2. UGG: Strengthen Winter Boots Positioning

UGG not appearing for general winter boots queries - Rufus recommends Timberland/Sorel instead.

  • Optimize listings for warmth, weather protection, and style keywords
  • Emphasize waterproofing and insulation specifications
  • Add structured data for winter boot attributes

3. TEVA: Expand Beyond Comparison Queries

TEVA only appears when directly compared to Chaco, missing from category discovery.

  • Build category presence for "hiking sandals", "water sandals", "travel sandals"
  • Ensure product titles include category keywords
  • Add use-case content (hiking, water activities, travel)

4. ALL BRANDS: Increase Amazon Sponsored Ads

Organic search visibility is critically low at 2% appearance rate.

  • Target category keywords: cushioned running shoes, winter boots, hiking sandals
  • Bid on medical/comfort keywords: plantar fasciitis, nurse shoes, knee pain
  • Maintain branded search presence while building category visibility
