What This Means for Deckers

The AI Search Landscape

Consumers increasingly discover and research products through AI-enhanced experiences:

  • Google AI Overviews: AI-generated summaries now appear at the top of Google search results for product queries
  • AI Chatbots: ChatGPT, Gemini, and Meta AI are becoming primary research tools for product recommendations
  • AI Search Engines: Perplexity and similar services provide direct answers instead of link lists

When AI systems recommend products, they draw from web content, reviews, expert publications, and structured data. Brands with strong digital authority show up more frequently and favorably.
