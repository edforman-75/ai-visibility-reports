Teva has solid presence in sandal-specific queries but is limited to that category.

What We Found

Where Teva Could Improve

Everyday Wear Positioning

AI systems only mention Teva for hiking and outdoor activities. There's no presence in casual, everyday sandal queries - a large market segment.

Lifestyle/Fashion

Teva isn't mentioned in style-conscious queries. Birkenstock dominates the "fashionable yet comfortable" sandal space.

Water Sports Beyond Sandals

Teva only appears in "sandals for water" queries, not broader water sports or beach activity queries.

Urban Outdoor

No visibility in city-to-trail or urban adventure queries. Teva is perceived as purely outdoor/wilderness.

Recommendations for Teva

Protect: Hiking Sandal Leadership

Teva shares the hiking sandal space with Chaco. Strengthen this position:

  • Create head-to-head comparison content (Teva vs Chaco)
  • Highlight water performance advantages
  • Feature trail running and hiking content

Expand: Everyday Wear

Build visibility for casual, daily use:

  • Create lifestyle content featuring everyday Teva styles

  • Position for city wear, not just trail use

Grow: Adventure Travel

Position Teva for the travel lifestyle market:

  • Highlight versatility for varied travel activities

Extend: Urban Outdoor

Target the city-to-trail lifestyle segment:
