Key Findings

1. Strong in Competitive Comparisons

When consumers ask "HUFT vs Petsy" or "HUFT vs Supertails," AI consistently positions HUFT favorably. 100% visibility in comparison queries.

2. Premium Brand Association

HUFT is mentioned alongside Royal Canin and Farmina - establishing premium positioning in AI responses.

3. Sara's Treats Breakthrough

Meta AI is the first surface to mention Sara's Treats sub-brand - a significant milestone for sub-brand visibility.

4. Location Queries Work Well

Delhi, Gurgaon, and other location-specific queries (67% visibility) show strong local presence.

5. Weak in Generic Category Queries

"Best dog food in India" rarely includes HUFT. Generic category searches favor global brands like Royal Canin and Pedigree.

Where ChatGPT Mentions HUFT
